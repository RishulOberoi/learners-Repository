How It Works

For Slide-Based Data:

Include these columns in your CSV/Excel:

  • Title: Slide title
  • Content: Slide content (will be auto-processed)
  • Image: (Optional) Image URL or path
For Tabular Data:

Any CSV/Excel file without Title/Content columns will be converted to a table slide.

AI Processing:
  • Short content (<80 chars) gets expanded
  • Long content (>100 chars) gets summarized
  • Medium content is used as-is
